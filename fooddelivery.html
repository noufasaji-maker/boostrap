Welcome to Zomato!

Zomato is an Indian multinational company and a food-tech ecosystem that provides online food ordering and delivery services, restaurant discovery, and dining-out options.

It is a comprehensive food-tech ecosystem connecting customers, restaurants, and delivery partners, providing services such as online food ordering, restaurant browsing with reviews, and discovering local eateries.

Users can browse these listings, place orders for delivery or pickup, and make payments through the Zomato app or website.
